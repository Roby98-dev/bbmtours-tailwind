Itinerary:

Day 1

09:00

Glamping Activitiy

Pick up at 09.00 and start to enjoy the glamping activitiy, glamping area will be in a quite place with green view in on a cliff with coffeee plantation surround. Not only coffee, there are also some fruits, such as; guava, jack fruit, Balinese orange, papaya, cassava, star fruit, banana, and others.

Day 2

09:00

Cycling

Have a cycling tour. After finish the tour, back to glamping area and taste Luwak Coffee. At evening, cooking a chicken with Balinese spice cooked in a bamboo. While cooking, enjoy the bonfire and have dinner together. At night, hunting Luwak in Coffee Plantation.

It starts at Kopi Luwak Landih, pass a challenging route and ends at Tukad Cepung Waterfall, get refreshment of young coconut. Continue to have a break and do a glamping in Lembah Halilintar.

Day 3

09:00

Cooking Activitiy

Making traditional breakfast (boiled cassava with honey and a cup of Luwak coffee). After that, transfer back to the next destination.
