Collect Memories in Undisan village

Kabupaten Bangli Bali.

Price:

$79/person

Trip Duration:

1 Day

Detail:

The activities that you follow this tour will be taught to learn to cook and follow the daily activities of Balinese people. Strong Points: Spent the night there will be more at people's houses as well as see and do activities together with local residents. Journeys and programs are particularly varied: rice fields, water fall, temples, villages, and a trek traversing the preserved forest of the island.

Itinerary:

Day 1

09:30 - 10:00

Trekking Around Village & Farming

Walking around and explore the countryside charms for fresh air breath-taking from the crowd of city is a good choice if you come and visit us in Undisan Village at D'Karang Homestay to get this activity. Walking through rice paddies and (IF POSSIBLE) participate a farming activity from cultivate the rice field by using cows up to planting the rice. Here you can feel to be a real farmer. After finishing the activity, you will have a break to enjoy refreshment which will be prepared with a traditional Balinese cake and fresh coconut fruit.

10:00 - 11:00

Visiting A Quite Waterfall

While you are trekking around the village, see how local people live their lives, you will be brought to a quite waterfall called Tangkup Waterfall. This waterfall is a small waterfall with empty people. You will enjoy the fresh air and fresh water.

11:00 - 13:00

Cooking Lesson

After trekking, you will enjoy cooking activity. Cooking lesson is an activity which brings you experiencing how to cook Balinese cuisine. Cooking is a joyful activity to do. The food that you cook together will be served for your lunch. As a starter activity to do in local house, you will learn a lot about how Balinese people process their foods in some traditional ways, e.g; mixing the condiments and meat in a stone with a hole inside and you will mix it by grinding using special wood for that.

13:00 - 13:30

Making Coconut Oil

While enjoying cooking lesson, you will know why Balinese traditional foods taste delicious and super yummy. One of the reason is because they are cooked by using oil from coconut which is processed directly by Balinese people. Here, you will be showed the process in making coconut oil, starting from how to open the coconut skin, scrape, squeeze out the coconut milk and cook with firewood. Functionality and usability: The original coconut oil can be used for massage therapy, treating burns, insect bites, and can also be drunk to treat of flatulence such as massage therapy, itching, subject to insect bites, and can be drunk for the treatment of flatulence.
