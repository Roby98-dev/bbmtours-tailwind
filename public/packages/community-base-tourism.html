The trip to Undisan Village will take you to the heart of Balinese culture while following the curved tracks, traversing the remote villages, exploring the sites well preserved from touristy industry and the most isolated places in the island of Gods. we will visit places of the rich Balinese cultures and many other places We discover also the sacred architecture and the sense of the indigenous religion while hiking through the villages with uncountable temples and while being present unexpectedly in the local party and procession. It is a complete and original voyage offering the real discovery of the Balinese. Between nature and culture, this easy level track circuit enable us to discover definitely the multiple hidden facets of the “real” Bali. and the activities that you follow this tour will be taught to learn to cook and follow the daily activities of Balinese people. Strong Points: Spent the night there will be more at people's houses as well as see and do activities together with local residents. Journeys and programs are particularly varied: rice fields, water fall, temples, villages, and a trek traversing the preserved forest of the island. The History, the religion, and the Balinese traditions are present in any instant of our long trips, there is a the possibility you will see the ceremony as an indirect be present in traditional customary feast (cremation or procession) during tours.
